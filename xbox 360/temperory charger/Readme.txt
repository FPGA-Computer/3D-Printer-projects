This is meant to be a temperory charger for the Xbox360 controller.
There are charge termination.

The battery pack is placed in the charger for an hour or so until 
the 360 controller recognizes the charge status to finish charging.

The metal contacts for the drill cradle base are from the metal strips 
that are used to crimp mesh onion bags.
